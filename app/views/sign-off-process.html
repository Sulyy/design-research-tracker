View our sign off process

Our sign-off process includes confirmation from UX and content designers, user researchers, delivery managers and product owners.

  1. UX and Content Designers confirm that the mockups meet the requirements and acceptance criteria set by the product owner.
  2. Any dependencies that relies on the wider team, buy-in is needed in order for the product owner to sign-off on the designs.
  3. We do user research and iterate to get sign-off on usability.
  4. We get product owner feedback and sign-off.
  5. The mockup / design is added into the tracker along with all of the user research insights and design justifications.
